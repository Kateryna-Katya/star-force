Star Force

Take control of powerful starships and lead your fleet into interstellar battles

Game Overview

Star Force is an explosive top-down space shooter that puts you in command of humanity’s last hope. Pilot high-tech ships across dangerous galaxies, where skill and reflexes are your best weapons.
With dozens of upgradeable spacecraft, bullet-hell levels, and massive boss battles, the game delivers thrilling arcade-style action.
Dodge meteors, survive laser storms, and destroy enemy fleets — every moment is packed with heart-pounding excitement!

FAQ

What kind of gameplay does Star Force offer?

Star Force is a fast-paced vertical scrolling shooter with explosive battles, powerful upgrades, and classic arcade mechanics.

Can I play Star Force without an internet connection?

Yes! Star Force supports offline gameplay so you can enjoy epic space battles anytime, anywhere.

How do I unlock new ships?

You earn credits and special parts during missions. Use them to unlock new starships with unique weapons and abilities.

Are there different difficulty modes?

The game features dynamic difficulty that increases as you progress, plus challenge modes for elite pilots.

Is there a story campaign?

Yes, Star Force includes a rich space campaign where you battle through different galaxies, defeat alien bosses, and uncover the secrets of a galactic war.
