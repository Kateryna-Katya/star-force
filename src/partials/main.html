Star Force

Take control of powerful starships and lead your fleet into interstellar battles

Game Overview

Star Force is an explosive top-down space shooter that puts you in command of humanity’s last hope. Pilot high-tech ships across dangerous galaxies, where skill and reflexes are your best weapons.
With dozens of upgradeable spacecraft, bullet-hell levels, and massive boss battles, the game delivers thrilling arcade-style action.
Dodge meteors, survive laser storms, and destroy enemy fleets — every moment is packed with heart-pounding excitement!

Highlights

  • Master the Shadows – Use stealth techniques to evade detection and strike at the right
    moment.

  • Dozens of Starships – Unlock and pilot a wide variety of ships, each equipped with distinct weapons and futuristic tech.

  • Space Campaign – Embark on an epic journey across hostile planets, asteroid fields, and fortified alien zones.

  • Classic Arcade Action – Fast-paced vertical shooter with modern polish

  • Boss Battles – Face off against massive enemies featuring dynamic, screen-filling attack patterns and intense visuals.

  • Offline Mode – Enjoy uninterrupted gameplay and galactic battles anytime, even without an internet connection.
